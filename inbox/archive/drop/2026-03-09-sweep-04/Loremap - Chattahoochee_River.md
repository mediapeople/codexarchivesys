## ❖ PHASE I: REVELATORY GATHERING

**Field Immersion**:  
The Chattahoochee winds like a soft spine through Georgia and Alabama—slow, sedimented, knowing. It drinks rain from the Appalachians and spills into the Gulf, a whispering current of old tongues and buried thresholds.

**Mythic Echo Harvesting**:

- Muscogee origins buried under concrete  
- Cold water releasing heat ghosts  
- Ruins of mill towns haunting its bends  
- Modern recreation layered atop ancestral ritual  
- Bridges as false crossings, dams as silencing engines
