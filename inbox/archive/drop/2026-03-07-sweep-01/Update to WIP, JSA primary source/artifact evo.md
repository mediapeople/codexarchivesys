# JSA Collage — Evolution Update

## Context

This document records the **first structural evolution** of the JSA collage artifact.

The collage remains in active work-in-progress state, but the composition has reached a moment where the structure feels coherent enough to document.

The goal of this update is to capture the artifact as it begins to stabilize.

---

# Structural Changes Observed

Several shifts occurred during this pass.

### Central Axis Clarification

The panel containing the Doctor Fate mask and the speech fragment:

> "Don't break out the party hats yet, whiz kid. We have a problem."

now sits closer to the center of the visual field.

This fragment functions as a narrative anchor.

The surrounding panels appear to radiate outward from it.

---

### Character Field Stabilization

Major figures now form a loose visual constellation:

- Wonder Woman (lower center)
- Golden Age Flash (lower right)
- Hawkman fragments
- civilian figure panels
- combat sequences
- Doctor Fate mask fragment

The composition now reads less like scattered fragments and more like a **compressed hero field**.

---

### Panel Energy Lines

Diagonal panel strips create directional motion across the page.

These lines now move through the composition in three dominant directions:

```
upper left → center
center → lower right
lower left → center
```

This creates a subtle visual vortex.

---

# Material Observations

The collage surface remains visibly layered.

Torn edges and overlapping paper produce shadow lines across the piece.

These shadows enhance the dimensional quality of the artifact.

No flattening pass has been attempted.

The dimensional surface is currently considered part of the aesthetic.

---

# Artifact Behavior

The piece is beginning to exhibit an interesting property.

Although the source panels originate from sequential comic storytelling, the collage now behaves more like a **simultaneous narrative field**.

Multiple moments exist at once.

Action fragments collide.

Characters appear outside of time.

This suggests the collage is evolving from illustration toward **myth compression**.

---

# Studio Observation

During documentation a repeated realization emerged.

The work process resembles **mining a narrative system for signal fragments**.

Comic panels are not treated as story units.

They become visual components in a new structure.

Extraction → recomposition → density.

---

# Artifact Status

Current stage:

**Structural Composition Achieved**

Possible next phases:

- edge trimming
- border framing
- mounting to archival board
- scanning for digital preservation
- integration into larger collage series

The piece remains open to further intervention.
