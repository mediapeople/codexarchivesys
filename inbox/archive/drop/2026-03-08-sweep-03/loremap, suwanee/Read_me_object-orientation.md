# ✦ Operator Note: Signals Within the Suwanee Loremap ✦

This note records the reasoning behind how certain objects — particularly **signals** — should interact with the **Suwanee Loremap**.

It exists to guide both human editors and assisting intelligences as the Codex grows.

---

## I. The Loremap as Terrain

The **Suwanee Loremap** defines a *terrain*.

It describes:

- environmental cues  
- mythic interpretation layers  
- symbolic structures within the place  

The loremap is therefore **not a container of content**, but a **map of a field**.

Other objects may exist **within the field**, but they do not alter the terrain definition unless the loremap itself is revised.
