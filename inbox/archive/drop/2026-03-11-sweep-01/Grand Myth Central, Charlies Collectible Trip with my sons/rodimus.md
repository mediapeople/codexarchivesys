# ✦ Rodimus Prime Field Acquisition ✦

Field site:  
Charlie's Collectibles.

Tables arranged like a quiet trading hall.  
Fluorescent lights humming overhead.  
Glass cases holding fragments of cultural memory.

I arrived tethered to the net  
through a pane of glass.

Laptop open.  
Codex system alive.

The archive watching itself  
being written.


## Artifact: Rodimus Prime

A small red warrior  
holding a burst of blue energy.

Rodimus is not merely a Transformer.

He is the myth of succession.

Hot Rod becomes Rodimus Prime  
when the burden of leadership  
is placed upon him.

The toy remembers the story  
even when the world forgets.


## Companion Artifact

A micro LEGO Falcon.

Tiny geometry of rebellion.

Studs and dish antenna  
suggesting a starship  
in pocket scale.

A reminder that myth can be built  
from very small parts.


## Field Context

This was not a solitary expedition.

I came here with my sons.

Collectibles halls are strange places.  
Half museum.  
Half market.  
Half time machine.

Parents bring children  
to touch the stories  
they once held in their own hands.

My boys moved slowly through the aisles,  
their pockets already emptied  
into the mythology.

They lingered anyway.


## Atmosphere

My wife’s voice arrived  
through the phone  
from somewhere in the sky channel.

A conversation with my youngest  
turned briefly toward a darker story —

a former NFL player  
whose life unraveled into homelessness  
and ended in violence.

The contrast landed strangely  
among shelves of plastic heroes.

Myth objects remain intact.

Human lives  
are more fragile.


## Additional Finds

Nearby, Megatron waited  
in a blister pack.

Dinobots guarded another shelf.

A strange council of machine creatures  
behind plastic windows.

Grand Myth Central  
in full operation.


## Operator Note

The Codex was open  
while the artifact was acquired.

Photographs taken.  
Object examined.  
Narrative captured.

The archive grows  
through moments like this.

Not planned.  
Not staged.

Just a small moment  
in a fluorescent-lit hall

where myth  
changes hands.
