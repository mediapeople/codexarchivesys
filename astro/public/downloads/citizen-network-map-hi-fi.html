✦ Citizen Node Doctrine ✦

Power inside a network is borrowed from citizens
and must be exercised with integrity.

Concept Nodes
Node
A point that receives and routes signal.
Human or technical.
Signal
Meaning moving through the network.
Speech · data · emotion · truth
Integrity
Alignment between received signal and routed signal.
No distortion. No reflex. No manipulation.
Agency
Capacity to act within the system.
Agency increases responsibility.
Permissions
Operational authority granted by the network.
Always conditional.
Citizen
The origin of trust. Citizens form the living substrate of the network.
All authority ultimately derives from them.
Bad Actors
Nodes that distort signal or abuse permissions.
They weaken trust and destabilize routing.
Powerless Nodes
Witness nodes without formal authority.
They preserve memory and protect truth at the edges.
Operational Order
Signal moving clearly · Authority moving carefully · Citizens remaining sovereign
