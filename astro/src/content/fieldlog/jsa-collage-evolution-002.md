This log records the second structural evolution pass of the JSA collage artifact.

The composition no longer depends on the framed speech fragment that anchored the previous state.

Instead, the piece is being driven by a denser lattice of thin diagonal shards that cross the whole field and create a more continuous impact structure.

Structural observations:
- the center now reads as a collision node rather than a dialogue panel
- thin strip fragments behave like braces or splinters spanning multiple figure clusters
- the Doctor Fate face on the left and the right-side hero grouping still hold the field in balance

Material observations:
- layered edges remain exposed and unflattened
- perimeter margins and irregular borders are still visible
- the surface continues to read as actively assembled rather than mounted or resolved

Studio behavior note:

This pass tests how much fracture the image can absorb before legibility starts to fail.
