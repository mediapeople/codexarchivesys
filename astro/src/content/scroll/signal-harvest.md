Crystal does not rush.

It forms in the dark, under conditions the eye cannot follow.

Pressure is not the enemy of the soft thing.
Pressure is how the soft thing learns what it is.

We have been collecting signals for years.
Not because we knew what to do with them.
Because they were there and we could not look away.

The harvest is not a moment.
The harvest is the ongoing act of noticing.

The archive is the barn.
What we call memory is the field.
