A website is not a page.
A body is not a feeling.
A system is not any single Tuesday.

The bones hold.
Not because they are extraordinary.
Because they were built for holding.

We are not trying to transcend the weight.
We are trying to remain accurate under it.

There is no cure.
There is maintenance.
There is the practice of continuing.

The archive records the practice.
Not the peak moments. Not the revelations.
The Tuesdays.

Especially the Tuesdays.
