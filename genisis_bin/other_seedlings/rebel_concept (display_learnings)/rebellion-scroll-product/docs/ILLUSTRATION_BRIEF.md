# Illustration Direction (Brief)

Make the scroll feel like a recovered constitutional artifact: sacred, engineered, legible.

Visual theses:
- THE VESSEL SERVES THE CARGO
- THE HUMAN IS THE NODE
- SYSTEMS ORBIT — THEY DO NOT POSSESS

Style:
- Monochrome linework, rare gold/rust accents
- Etched plate / field manual / stamp language
- Sparse, diagrammatic, ceremonial
