The stairwell between floors three and four always sounds different before rain.
Tonight it sounded padded, like the building was holding its breath.

I kept thinking about the archive:
not the files, the handoff.
What is legible at 2am and still legible six months later.

If we keep polishing the interface and stop labeling the bins,
we will lose the work politely.
