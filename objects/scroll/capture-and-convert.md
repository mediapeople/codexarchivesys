Signal arrives first.

Not as language  
but as pressure.

Something enters the body  
before the mind organizes it.

A flicker.  
A compression.  
A shape forming inside noise.

To capture  
is not to own.

It is to notice.

To convert  
is not to distort.

It is to translate  
between layers of reality.

The operator stands between them.

Listening.

Holding the fragment long enough  
for it to become form.
