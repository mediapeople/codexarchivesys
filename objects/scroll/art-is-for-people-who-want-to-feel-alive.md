Art is for people
who want
to feel
alive-

not enter-
tained,
not dis-
tracted,
not im-
proved-

a-
live.

Pulse
in the
wrists.

Heat
behind
the eyes.

Sweat
on the
hips-

salt
and
gravity
leaning.

Something
un-
purchased
mov-
ing
under
the ribs.

Art is for
the ones
who sus-
pect
this
is
not
enough-

and dare
to
say
so.
