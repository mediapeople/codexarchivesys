## Core Operating Model

1. Content types are structural bones.
2. Navigation is orientation infrastructure.
3. Field maps act as contracts across content, design, and system.
4. Messaging frames assign rhetorical roles to fields.
5. Communication style is governed per field and per context.
6. Node context (type, audience, journey position, mission) determines rendering behavior.
7. Editors are the final argument-makers at publish time.
